## Email Sending Tips

1. **Personalize:** Always use customer's actual name
2. **Plain Text:** Use plain text emails (more trusted, less spam-flagged)
3. **Clear Subject:** Make it obvious what the email is about
4. **One CTA:** Focus on one main action per email
5. **Mobile-Friendly:** Keep lines short (< 60 characters)
6. **Response Time:** Aim for < 24h on support emails
7. **Signature:** Include your real name and contact info
